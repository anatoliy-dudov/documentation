# Frequently Asked Questions

## Billing

??? question "How do I figure out how many seats I need?"
    Every user with the **Member** or **Administrator** role takes up a
    seat. Users with the **Guest** role (access limited to specific
    projects) don't count toward the seat limit and aren't billed.

??? question "What happens if I invite a new employee mid-month?"
    The cost of the new seat is prorated for the remaining days of the
    billing period — the additional charge appears on your next invoice,
    not immediately.

??? question "Can I upgrade from Business to Enterprise without losing data?"
    Yes, upgrading a plan never deletes or archives data. Downgrading a
    plan may restrict access to features unavailable on the new plan
    (e.g., SSO), but it does not delete the underlying data.

## Access permissions

??? question "What's the difference between 'Workspace Admin' and 'Project Admin'?"
    A Workspace Admin manages billing, SSO, and all projects. A Project
    Admin only manages settings and members for a specific project, with
    no access to billing or SSO.

??? question "Can a Guest see other projects in the workspace?"
    No. The Guest role is limited to the specific projects they were
    explicitly invited to, and has no visibility into the workspace's
    other projects.

??? question "How do I transfer workspace ownership to someone else?"
    Under **Workspace Settings → Members**, the current owner has a
    **Transfer ownership** action. Once the new owner confirms, the
    previous owner is automatically assigned the Administrator role —
    access isn't lost, only ownership.
